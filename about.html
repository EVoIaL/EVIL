About EVIL

Ethical Vanguards of Integrity and Liberty (EVIL) was founded to champion the rights of individuals who are unable to advocate for themselves. We are a group of committed individuals who believe in the importance of prioritizing the well-being of the most vulnerable members of our society.

Our organization was built on the core principles of empathy, science, and individual liberty. We tirelessly work to ensure that the elderly, infants, and disabled receive the care and support they need, as determined by the best available scientific and psychological research. In doing so, we aim to create a more just and compassionate world.

EVIL's patrons are known for their unwavering dedication to ethical principles, even in the face of popular opinion. We believe that every individual deserves the right to live a life free from unnecessary suffering and that it is our responsibility to help those who cannot help themselves.
