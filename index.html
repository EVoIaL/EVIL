Welcome to EVIL

Ethical Vanguards of Integrity and Liberty (EVIL) is a dedicated organization focused on advocating for the human rights of individuals who are unable to advocate for themselves, such as the elderly, infants, and disabled. Our mission is to provide support in accordance with the best available scientific and psychological research while valuing individual freedoms.
